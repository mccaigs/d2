Contracting authority: Eastborough Council.
RFP for a resident engagement portal and support service.
The supplier must provide a hosted service for resident messaging, reporting, and service requests.
The buyer requires implementation support and monthly service reporting.
Responses must include a signed declaration, pricing schedule, insurance certificate, cyber security policy, and accessibility statement.
Pass/fail checks apply to mandatory documents, eligibility, insurance levels, and data protection policy evidence.
The tender asks bidders to demonstrate experience, but the available brief does not include case studies, named references, certifications, or approved proof points.
Submission is via the council procurement portal by 30 June.
Evaluation will score compliance first, then delivery quality and commercial pricing.
