Buyer: West Mercia Integrated Care Board.
Tender notice for digital referral and case management services.
The supplier must provide a secure SaaS platform for referral triage, case tracking, reporting, and workflow management.
The solution shall include implementation methodology, mobilisation planning, service reporting, SLA management, and KPI dashboards.
The supplier must demonstrate data protection controls, cyber security governance, GDPR compliance, quality assurance, and risk management.
The buyer requires case study evidence from comparable public sector services, named delivery roles, user training, and support arrangements.
The response should show social value evidence, accessibility compliance, benefits tracking, and value for money.
Responses must include a pricing schedule, signed declaration, insurance certificate, implementation plan, risk register, and data security policy.
Submission is through the procurement portal by 12 May at 12:00.
Evaluation will consider requirement coverage, evidence strength, service delivery quality, compliance readiness, and commercial pricing.
